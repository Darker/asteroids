Stavy:
$$
[x, y, \varphi, v_x, v_y, v_{\varphi}]
$$

Vstupy:
$$
[F, M]
$$

Parametry:
$$
[m, J]
$$


Stavovy popis:
$$
\dot{x} = v_x \\
\dot{y} = v_y \\
\dot{\varphi} = v_{\varphi} \\
\dot{v}_x = \frac{1}{m}F\cos(\varphi) \\
\dot{v}_y = \frac{1}{m}F\sin(\varphi) \\
\dot{v}_{\varphi} = \frac{1}{J}M \\
$$
